Our Mission

NEU Chinese Language Table is a new student organization which allows students studying or familiar with Mandarin to practice their conversational skills with both peers of similar proficiencies and native speakers. Our events offer a casual environment in which to learn and opportunities to taste a variety of authentic Chinese foods. Students studying formally in classes, heritage speakers, native/fluent speakers and those self-studying the language are all invited to attend! Come see what we're all about! 跟我们聊天吧。 下个星期见！

Events

Dumpling Making

We hand-make tasty 饺子 (dumplings) in the AAC! We make both vegetarian and meat 饺子. We prepare the filling ahead of time and wrap with dumpling skins during the event. Then we steam and eat! 很好吃! Check out one of our dumpling nights in the video below: We usually plan one dumpling-making night every semester!




Asian American Day

On Saturday September 10th, we went to volunteer at the 6th Asian American day in Boston Common! There were music and dancing performances, yummy food vendors, and bubble tea!

Study Abroad Opportunities

Critical Language Scholarship

CLS Program: The Critical Language Scholarship (CLS) Program is an intensive overseas language and cultural immersion program for American students enrolled at U.S. colleges and universities. The program includes intensive language instruction and structured cultural enrichment experiences designed to promote rapid language gains.
