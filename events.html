Events

A brief glimpse of our legacy:

Kickoff Day

Our very first event was on February 20th. We ordered lunch from Sunrise Cafe and ate it in the Forsyth building while conversing in Chinese. It was a lot of fun and we had a great turnout. About 20 people showed up for our first table, and it was a blast!

A Bite of China

On April 4th, we hosted a dinner while watching the show "A Bite of China," a film exploring China's illustrious history of food, in terms of both cooking and eating.

Dumpling Making Night

On Monday April 11th we hand-made tasty 饺子 (dumplings) in the AAC! We made both vegetarian and meat 饺子。
