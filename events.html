Events

A brief glimpse of our legacy: Spring 2016

Kickoff Day

Our very first event was on February 20th. We ordered lunch from Sunrise Cafe and ate it in the Forsyth building while conversing in Chinese. It was a lot of fun and we had a great turnout. About 20 people showed up for our first table, and it was a blast!

A Bite of China

On April 4th, we hosted a dinner while watching the show "A Bite of China," a film exploring China's illustrious history of food, in terms of both cooking and eating.

Dumpling Making Night

On Monday April 11th we hand-made tasty 饺子 (dumplings) in the AAC! We made both vegetarian and meat 饺子。

6th Annual Asian American Day

On Saturday September 10th, we went to volunteer at the 6th Asian American day in Boston Common! There were music and dancing performances, yummy food vendors, and bubble tea!




A brief glimpse of our legacy: Fall 2016

First event of Fall 2016!

Our first event was on Monday (October 3rd) at 7 pm in Snell 031. For the first half hour, we had a mini meeting about logicistics and plans for the whole semester and positions available on the eBoard! We introduced ourselves (Using Chinese of course!). Then we took a trip to ChaTime for some bubble tea!.

October 31st - Halloween!

On Halloween, we met at 7 pm in Snell 031 to make Caramel Apples. We heated the caramel, dipped the apples, sprinkled them with goodies, and learned related Chinese vocabulary! 非常好吃！
